How to rent easy with LeafMobil

Located in the city of Montreal, we offer a wide range of electric cars for rent.

You can rent for an hour or several days. Choose to pick up the car immediately or reserve ahead of time. When you're done, the car is returned to the space it belongs to.

Why download LeafMobil? SJoin for free with no monthly fee It costs nothing to create a user and you only pay when you rent a car.

If you have any questions, please contact us in the contact section. To reserve, find the one closest to you in the interactive map and submit your request.
